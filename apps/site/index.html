Powerful Features For Sales Professionals

Everything you need to stay on top of your sales performance and commissions in one intuitive platform.

Real-Time Commission Tracking

Employees see their earnings update as deals close, or auto adjust when deals unwind. No more waiting for monthly statements to know what they've earned.

Performance Analytics

Visualize your sales trends, identify your best sales periods, and optimize your strategy with data-driven insights.

Personalized Dashboard

Customize your view to focus on the metrics that matter most to you. Track specific deal types, finance products, or time periods.

Secure & Confidential

Your data is encrypted and protected. Employees can see their personal earnings without exposing their information to others.

Mobile Access

Employees can check their commissions on the go with our mobile-friendly interface. Perfect for busy sales professionals working the lot.

Commission Alerts

Employees receive notifications when new commissions are posted or when they hit your sales targets. Helps them stay motivated with real-time feedback.

How MyShareNow Works

Simple, seamless integration with your dealership's existing systems for effortless commission tracking.

Connect Your DMS

MyShareNow integrates with all major Dealer Management Systems to securely access your sales data without disrupting your workflow.

Customize Your Pay Plans

Set up commission structure, pay plans, and bonus thresholds to ensure accurate tracking of your employees earnings.

Track In Real-Time

Provide access to Sales teams so they can see commissions update as deals are processed. See front-end, back-end, and bonus earnings clearly broken down.

Analyze & Optimize

Use insights from your sales patterns to identify opportunities, simulate different pay plans, and focus your efforts on the most profitable activities.

AI & Automation

Use our AI based pay plan generator that uses insights into your data to create new pay plans and automatically or with a button-click apply those improvements monthly.
